Federal Government Programmes

Active FGN programmes supporting Nigerian farmers in 2025.

FEDERAL

Anchor Borrowers Programme (ABP)

CBN-backed loans for smallholder farmers cultivating key commodities like rice, maize, wheat, and cotton. Repayable in-kind at harvest.

Apply via CBN / Your Bank
FEDERAL

Presidential Fertilizer Initiative (PFI)

Blended NPK and Urea fertilisers subsidised by FGN. Available through ADP offices and licensed agro-dealers.

Visit your State ADP
FEDERAL

National Agricultural Growth Scheme (NAGS)

Provides improved seeds, inputs, and extension services to boost productivity. Targeting 5 million farmers nationally.

Contact FMA&RD
FEDERAL

Youth in Agriculture (YAGEP)

Startup grants, training, land access, and mentorship for youth farmers aged 18-35 across Nigeria.

Apply via FMYSD
CBN

AgriSME Fund (BOA)

Bank of Agriculture provides soft loans to registered agricultural businesses at single-digit interest rates.

Apply at Bank of Agriculture
REGULATORY

NAFDAC Certification

All processed food products for sale must comply with NAFDAC standards. Registration required before commercial distribution.

Visit nafdac.gov.ng
INSURANCE

NAIS - Agricultural Insurance

NAIC provides crop and livestock insurance with subsidised premiums for smallholder farmers registered with state ADPs.

Contact NAIC office
EXTENSION

ADP Extension Services

State ADPs provide free extension officers who visit farms, advise on best practices, pest control, and input sourcing.

Contact your State ADP
