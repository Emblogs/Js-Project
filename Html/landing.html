What We Offer

Everything Your Farm
Needs in One App

From planting to harvest, from livestock to land sales — NaijaFarm covers every corner of your farm operations.

Crops Tracker

Record planting dates and watch a live progress bar count down to harvest. Track cassava, maize, yam, rice, vegetables and more across multiple fields.

Livestock Manager

Track every cow, goat, sheep, poultry bird and fish by age, breed, health status and purchase price. Know exactly what you own at any time.

Worker Records

Manage all your farm hands, supervisors and drivers. Track wages, roles, tenure, state of origin and automatically calculate your monthly payroll.

Farm Market

Post listings to buy or sell farm land, livestock, tractors, and equipment. Filter by category and contact sellers directly by phone.

Government Support

Browse active FGN agricultural programmes — ABP, fertilizer subsidies, youth grants and insurance. Get personalized recommendations based on your state and farm type.

Works Offline

All your data is saved directly in your browser. No internet needed after setup, no subscription fees, no server — your records stay with you.

How It Works

Simple as ABC

Get started in minutes. No technical knowledge required — if you can use a phone, you can use NaijaFarm.

1

Create Your Account

Sign up with a username and password. It takes less than 30 seconds — no email or phone number needed.

2

Add Your Farm Data

Enter your crops, animals, workers and market listings. The app guides you through every step clearly.

3

Track Everything
